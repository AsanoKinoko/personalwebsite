Password Rules

  • At least 8 and at most 50 characters in length
  • At least one lower-case letter (a-z)
  • At least one upper-case letter (A-Z)
  • At least one digit (0-9)
  • At least one and at most ten non alpha-numeric character(s) (Example: @, #, $)
  • Should not have three or more consecutive characters that match any portion of the user email address

Caesar Cipher Rules & Guide

  • Enter a phrase to encrypt it into a password, or input encrypted text to decrypt it back
  • Specify a custom shift step (default is 3 if empty or not set)
  • Letters (A-Z, a-z) will be shifted forward (encrypt) or backward (decrypt)
  • Numbers, spaces, and special characters remain unchanged
  • Perfect for creating memorable but encrypted passwords
